Our Mission: Empower Frontline Teams with Real-time Intelligence.

CloudTAK is developed to augment the already powerful Team Awareness Kit (TAK) ecosystem.

CloudTAK provides browser based geospatial tools for use by operational staff supporting existing TAK users in the field. To do this we have brought together a group of engineers, developers, and subject matter experts to create a cloud-native solution that enhances the TAK experience with real-time data sharing, cross-platform interoperability, and secure communication.

The best part? We offer this solution as an open-source project, ensuring that it remains accessible to all who need it, without the burden of licensing fees.

Powered by CloudTAK

CloudTAK integrates the proven capabilities of the Team Awareness Kit (TAK) with a resilient, high-performance cloud backbone. This enables:

  • Real-time Synchronization: Instantly update and share geospatial data, chat, and other critical information across all connected devices.
  • Flexible Deployment: Deploy CloudTAK rapidly in various operational environments, leveraging existing network infrastructure or small, incident-deployed servers.
  • Open and Extensible: Built on an open-source architecture, CloudTAK supports custom plugins and integrations, allowing you to tailor the system to your specific mission requirements.
  • Enhanced Security: Secure by design, CloudTAK ensures your operational data is protected with robust encryption and access controls.
