Powered by Cloud-Optimized TAK.

CloudTAK integrates the proven capabilities of the Team Awareness Kit (TAK) with a resilient, high-performance cloud backbone. This enables:

  • Real-time Synchronization: Instantly update and share geospatial data, chat, and other critical information across all connected devices.
  • Flexible Deployment: Deploy CloudTAK rapidly in various operational environments, leveraging existing network infrastructure or small, incident-deployed servers.
  • Open and Extensible: Built on an open-source architecture, CloudTAK supports custom plugins and integrations, allowing you to tailor the system to your specific mission requirements.
  • Enhanced Security: Secure by design, CloudTAK ensures your tactical data is protected with robust encryption and access controls.
